Our History

We started in 2015 and quickly established ourself as one of the leading blogging company and since then we have produced many great bloggers. At first this company was started by two brothers with having a vision of empowering the world with blogs by providing each individual a global platform to share their thoughts on the current situation of world. Now we have more than 2,000 employes and around 10 offices spreading over the globe.

  • Owner
  • CEO
  • CTO
  • CMO

Rohit and Mohit Aggarwal are the two brothers started their journey as computer science students in college. On 2005 they made a small thought share platform for college where every student can write and post articles on their favourite topics and present their views over the situation. This small project soon starts getting attention from many other colleges and gradually becomes a global platform.

Suresh Sen was the batchmate of Mohit Aggarwal and was the first person to join WordBlog. He has contributed significantly in developing WordBlog with new innovative steps. He has added many feature to WordBlog like Onsite blogging, Blogging with friends etc.

Rahul Singh is a brilliant engineer who was previously working as project manager in a different tech company. He is a curious and a fascinating guy with a hunger for new technology and innovation. He has helped in building and enhancing the user interface and serverside development.

Rishav Arya was a senior of Mohit and helped him to spread his small project to many colleges. He was the second guy to join WordBlog. He also helped in hiring many new workers and is the man behind making this a global platform through his marketing skills.
